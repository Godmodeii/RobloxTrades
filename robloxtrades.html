How RobloxTrades Works

A transparent, crypto-powered P2P marketplace for Roblox items

1️⃣
Create Account
Sign up for free. Add your crypto wallet addresses to receive payments from buyers.
2️⃣
List Your Item
Post your Roblox item with details, price in USD (shown in crypto), and your accepted coins.
3️⃣
Buyer Orders
A buyer places an order — a private chat opens between you and the buyer automatically.
4️⃣
Chat & Coordinate
Use the chat to share wallet addresses, agree on crypto, and confirm payment was sent.
5️⃣
Deliver the Item
Once you confirm the crypto payment, deliver the Roblox item to the buyer in-game.
6️⃣
Leave a Review
Both parties rate the transaction to build trust and reputation on the platform.
🔒 Security Notice: RobloxTrades does NOT hold funds. All crypto transactions are peer-to-peer directly between wallets. Always verify item delivery before confirming. Never pay outside the agreed chat channel. Chargeback scams are reportable.
